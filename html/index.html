Styllex

Styllex, a styling and beauty app focused on grooming for men and women. It is a market place that brings together barbers and beauty personnel, which allows customers to locate the best hair and beauty stylist available.

Why Stylleex?

It Saves Time

Book their appointments and be able to estimate how long it requires for their order to be fulfilled and the time for their service to be rendered.

Price Accuracy

The price ranges are displayed which allows customers to work with a budget and estimate the amount that will be spent.

How to Book an Appointment

1. Sign Up
2. Enter Details
3. Register
4. Choose Styles/Stylist
5. Select Shop
6. Book Appointment
